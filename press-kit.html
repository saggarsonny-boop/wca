About Dr. Sonny Saggar

Dr. Sonny Saggar spent 25 years in emergency medicine and was trained at Oxford University and Barts Hospital in London. After navigating the federal criminal justice system firsthand, he founded White Collar Academy to provide practical, evidence-based guidance for defendants, families, and attorneys.

Key Statistics & Data Points

112+ Federal Prisons: WCA maps custody guidelines across all active BOP camps, low, medium, and high complexes.
150,000+ Inmates: The federal prison population currently standing inside the BOP.
The Stack: Collateral professional consequences (licensing boards, HHS-OIG exclusion, DEA bans) outlast active court-ordered sentences.
