TL;DR Summary

This interactive timeline maps the key phases of the federal criminal justice system—from investigation through supervised release—providing critical action items for each step.

Federal Process Timeline

Explore each phase of the federal case progression. Click steps to understand requirements.

1. Investigation

Federal agency inquiries (FBI, IRS, HHS-OIG). Focus on target letters and retaining counsel.

2. Indictment / Information

Formal charges filed. Decisions regarding plea agreements or proceeding to trial.

3. Presentence Investigation (PSR)

The probation interview determines security points, medical history, and custody options.

4. Sentencing Hearing

The judge consults the advisory guidelines and individual mitigation variables to issue the sentence.

5. Self-Surrender & Custody

Designation to a BOP facility. Program options (like RDAP) and camp life adjustments.

6. Reentry & Supervision

Supervised release phase. Rebuilding professional licenses, credentials, and credits.
