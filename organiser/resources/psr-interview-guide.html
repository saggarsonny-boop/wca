Preparing for the Presentence (PSR) Interview

Understand what to expect, how to present yourself, and critical questions to prepare for.

1. What to Expect

The Probation Officer conducts this interview to write the Presentence Report (PSR), which directly influences the judge's sentencing decisions and BOP placements. The interview covers family history, financial status, physical health, and your account of the offense.

2. Key Questions to Prepare For

  • "Can you explain your involvement in the offense?"
  • "What steps have you taken to make things right?"
  • "How has this case affected your family?"

3. How to Present Yourself

Be respectful, answer honestly, show clear remorse, and dress in professional attire. Do not make excuses for the offense; take complete responsibility.
