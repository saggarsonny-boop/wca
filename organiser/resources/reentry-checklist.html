Reentry & Transition Checklist

Essential action items to restore credit, restore professional credentials, and find stable housing.

1. Credit Repair & Financial Setup

  • Request free credit reports to check for errors.
  • Open a secured credit card to begin rebuilding credit scores.
  • Establish direct relationships with local bank representatives.

2. Professional Licensing Restoration

  • Identify state board rules regarding background records.
  • Prepare written explanation letters summarizing rehabilitation steps.

3. Job Search & Housing Options

  • Build a resume emphasizing transferable skills.
  • Source transitional housing options and local apartments.
