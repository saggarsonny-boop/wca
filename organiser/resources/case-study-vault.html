Case Study #108: Medical Professional Licensure Restoration

Charge: Healthcare Fraud (18 U.S.C. § 1347)
Mitigation Strategy: Clinical mitigation logs demonstrating complete restitution steps and voluntary recovery program enrollment prior to indictment.
Outcome: Sentence variance of 18 months below guidelines range. Licensing board reinstatement approved with monitoring conditions.

Case Study #224: Non-Violent First-Time Offender RDAP Placement

Charge: Wire Fraud (18 U.S.C. § 1343)
Mitigation Strategy: Accurate mapping of substance abuse history inside the draft Presentence Report (PSR).
Outcome: Verified RDAP eligibility resulting in 12 months sentence reduction and early release to halfway house.
