Support Group & Program Directory

A list of national and local groups assisting inmates and families during the transition process.

1. National Organizations

  • Families Against Mandatory Minimums (FAMM): Advocacy group focused on sentencing reform.
  • Prison Fellowship: Faith-based reentry programs and support groups for inmates and families.

2. Reentry Resources

  • National Reentry Resource Center: Database of local housing, job placement, and training opportunities.
  • Local Reentry Coalitions: State-level networks facilitating transition services.
