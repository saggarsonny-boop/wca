Frequently Asked Questions

Can I share this workspace with my defense attorney?

Yes. The Case Organiser helps you organize facts, documents, and dates. By presenting structured, chronologically accurate files to your lawyer, you save them hours of basic administrative work, directly reducing your billable legal fees.

What is the Presentence Report (PSR) Mitigation Workbook?

The PSR is the single most important document in a federal case, determining your security level and release eligibility. The Organiser includes a structured mitigation section to help you gather character letters, draft your personal statement, and prepare for your probation interview, replacing the need for expensive external consultants.

How does the First Step Act and RDAP Calculator work?

It calculates your projected release timeline based on your offense type, sentencing date, and eligible programming credits (such as RDAP or FSA credits), giving you and your family clear milestones.

Is my data secure if my trial expires or payment lapses?

Yes. Your data is fully encrypted and stored securely. If your trial ends or your subscription pauses, we do not delete or purge your files. Your workspace is simply locked until you choose to reactivate.

I am not indicted but am under audit or investigation. Is this useful?

Yes. The early stage of an investigation is when documentation is most critical. Having a private, encrypted space to log dates, store communications, and map out facts helps you protect your timeline before an indictment ever occurs.
