# White Collar Academy - Media Outreach Assets

This document contains outreach templates, target media outlets, and pitch frameworks for Dr. Sonny Saggar and White Collar Academy.

---

## 1. Target Media Outlets

### Legal Publications
- **Law360:** Reaches corporate litigation attorneys and defense partners.
- **Law.com / National Law Journal:** Covers federal trial trends and sentencing.
- **ABA Journal:** Focuses on attorney tools, client intake, and professional standards.

### Medical Publications
- **MedPage Today / KevinMD:** High reach among physicians and clinical professionals. Focuses on licensing board exclusion and professional restoration.

### General News & Investigative Outlets
- **Associated Press (AP) / Reuters:** Covers federal sentencing reform and prison conditions.
- **Wall Street Journal (WSJ):** Focuses on white-collar defense, corporate enforcement, and reentry challenges for white-collar professionals.

## 2. Press Release Template

```
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

OXFORD-TRAINED PHYSICIAN LAUNCHES 'WHITE COLLAR ACADEMY' TO HELP LICENSED PROFESSIONALS NAVIGATE THE FEDERAL JUSTICE SYSTEM

[Date] — White Collar Academy (WCA) has officially launched its online case management platform and book series, "The Road," designed to guide doctors, lawyers, and financial professionals through the complex federal criminal justice process.

Founded by Dr. Sonny Saggar, an Oxford-trained emergency medicine physician and federal system survivor, WCA combines clinical trauma response frameworks with lived experience to map the entire journey from initial indictment to post-custody release.

"Most defendants face the court completely blind to the collateral consequences," says Dr. Saggar. "The sentence is only part of the impact. Professionals face a 'stack' of administrative actions—DEA exclusions, licensing suspensions, and database exclusions—that outlast their active supervision. We built WCA to provide a plain-English map so families and defendants don't have to walk this road blind."

WCA features:
1. The Road Book Series: A 26-chapter guide outlining sentencing zones and custody points.
2. Case Organizer: An interactive mitigation workbook, attorney portal, and sentencing calculator.
3. Prison Library Sponsorship: Program donating book copies directly to federal BOP facilities.

For media inquiries, interview bookings, or review copies, contact press@whitecollaracademy.com.
```
